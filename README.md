# USHA-STORES-ANNUAL-REPORT-INTERACTIVE-DASHBOARD

DESCRIPTION:
Usha Stores Annual Report Interactive Dashboard is an interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze and visualize Usha Stores' annual sales performance for 2025. The dashboard consolidates transactional order data and presents key business metrics, trends, customer behavior, channel performance, product insights, and geographical sales distribution through Pivot Tables, charts, slicers, and KPI summaries.

OBJECTIVE:
The primary objectives of this dashboard are:
-> Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
-> Track monthly sales trends and order volumes.
-> Evaluate state-wise sales contribution.
-> Measure product category performance.
-> Support data-driven business decisions through visual analytics.

TECH STACK:
-> Microsoft Excel – Primary platform used for data analysis, reporting, and dashboard development.
-> Pivot Tables – Used for data aggregation, summarization, and multidimensional analysis.
-> Pivot Charts – Enabled dynamic visualization of sales, customer, and product performance metrics.
-> Slicers – Provided interactive filtering capabilities for enhanced dashboard usability.
-> Data Cleaning & Transformation – Performed data validation, formatting, categorization, and preprocessing to ensure data accuracy and consistency.
-> Excel Functions & Formulas – Utilized functions such as IF, SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP, and TEXT functions for calculations and data manipulation.
-> Dashboard Design & KPI Visualization – Developed an interactive executive dashboard with key performance indicators (KPIs), trend analysis, and business insights.
-> Data Modeling & Reporting – Structured data to support efficient reporting, performance tracking, and decision-making.

DATA SOURCE:
The dataset was obtained from a YouTube tutorial project and contains e-commerce sales data, including orders, customers, products, sales channels, and order status information. Referrence: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gTK5rNhWJyA&t=1671s

FEATURES:
-> It evaluate overall business growth  by sales performance analysis through monthly sales trends, order volume tracking, and revenue monitoring
-> It offers better customer preferences by visualizing gender-wise sales distribution, age group segmentation, and purchasing behavior to 
-> It enables product performance evaluation through category-wise sales analysis, product size distribution, and quantity sold metrics.
-> It measures channel effectiveness by comparing sales performance across Amazon, Myntra, Ajio, and other online marketplaces.
-> It delivers geographic insights through state-wise sales distribution and regional performance comparisons to identify key revenue-generating locations.

HIGHLIGHTS:
-> Interactive filtering using slicers.
-> One-page executive dashboard for quick decision-making.
-> Automated aggregation through Pivot Tables.

BUSINESS IMPACT:
-> Regional Growth Planning: Identifies top-performing and underperforming states, enabling location-specific sales and marketing strategies.
-> Marketing Effectiveness: Supports targeted marketing campaigns by highlighting the most profitable customer segments, channels, and geographic regions.
-> Revenue Optimization: Identifies high-performing sales channels, products, and regions, enabling focused investment and growth strategies.
-> Inventory Management: Helps forecast demand and optimize stock levels by analyzing category-wise and size-wise sales trends.
-> Operational Efficiency: Helps reduce operational bottlenecks.order fulfillment performance through delivery, return, refund, and cancellation metrics.
